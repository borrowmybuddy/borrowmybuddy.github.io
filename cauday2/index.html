How does it work?

We match up pet owners with those seeking some fluffy companionship. So far, we have everything from alpacca to goldfish available for borrowing. We even accept snakes, children, turtles, kittens (no cats), roosters, and cows. We guarantee your pet will have so much fun with its temporary buddy, it won't want to come back home to you!
